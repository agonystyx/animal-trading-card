Dumbo Octopus

The large ear-like fins resemble the wing-like ears of Disney's flying elephant Dumbo, and give the species its name.

  • Scientific Name: Grimpoteuthis
  • Average Length: 25 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 4 years
  • Habitat: Extreme depths below sea-level

These are some of the deepest dwelling animals in the ocean. When spread out, its eight arms form a skirt-like shape around its body, not unlike the ghost villains from the video game Pac-Man.
